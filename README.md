# 🎙️ Voice Karar

> Speak. Confirm. Trust.

## 📖 About

Voice Karar is an AI-powered platform that helps MSMEs convert informal verbal business agreements into structured and digitally confirmed agreements.

Business owners continue making deals over normal phone calls or in-person conversations. After the discussion, they simply record a short voice summary. Voice Karar extracts important business details using AI, generates a structured agreement, and sends it to the other party for confirmation.

---

## 🚀 Features (MVP)

-  Voice Summary Recording
-  Speech-to-Text
-  Multilingual Support (Hindi, Marathi, English, etc.)
-  AI Agreement Extraction
-  AI Follow-up Questions for Missing Information
-  Structured Agreement Generation
-  Shareable Agreement Link
-  Buyer/Supplier Confirmation
-  Agreement History
